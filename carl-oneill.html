About

A teacher first, in a sector that needs good ones.

I've spent my career in two places at once — out in aged care and disability services, and at the front of the classroom. That overlap is the whole point. I know what compliant, high-quality care actually looks like on the floor, and I know how to teach people to deliver it.

As a National Learning & Development Manager I've built and run training for more than 1,500 staff — dementia care, behaviour support, crisis intervention, case management — and managed the systems that keep it all compliant and on the record. I coach, I mentor, and I'm a firm believer that learning never really finishes.

Training development, compliance, resilience and leadership — used to help teams provide care worth being proud of.
